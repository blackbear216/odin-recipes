The Classic

Ingredients

  • Brown Pasta
  • Sweet Potato
  • Broccoli
  • Cabbage
  • Baked Beans

Steps

  1. Weigh 75g~ of broccoli and 75g~ of cabbage
  2. Wash instruments and green vegetables
  3. Boil a litre of water in kettle while chopping
  4. Pour hot water into pot and turn hob up to max heat
  5. Put vegetables in steamer on top of pot and put lid on
  6. Once lid starts rattling turn heat down to minimum
  7. Wash and chop 200g~ of sweet potato into moderate chunks
  8. Weigh 75g~ of dry brown pasta
  9. Put sweet potato and pasta in same bowl
  10. Clean up chopping board and knife
  11. Take steamer off pot and empty sweet potato + pasta into pot of water
  12. Put steamer and lid back on top of pot and turn heat up to max
  13. Wait for pot to rattle then turn down to minimum
  14. Wait 10-12 minutes for food to cook
  15. In meantime open can of baked beans, empty half into tupperware for next time
  16. Keep other half of beans in can for now
  17. When cooking time is up turn hob off
  18. Empty greens into a bowl then use the steamer as a drainer for sweet potato and pasta
  19. Put sweet potato and pasta on a plate
  20. Empty beans from can (yes cold) onto the plate
  21. Eat
