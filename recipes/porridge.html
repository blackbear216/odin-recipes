Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Soy Milk
  • Water
  • Apple
  • Cinnamon
  • Ground Linseed
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Wash tools and apple
  2. Add 100g oats
  3. Add 15g~ ground linseed
  4. Add 300ml~ soy milk
  5. Add a few hundred mls more of water to taste
  6. Chop apple into small chunks and add to bowl
  7. Add a small spoonful of peanut butter
  8. Add a moderate sprinkle of cinnamon
  9. Stir and put in fridge over night
