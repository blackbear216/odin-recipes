Ingredients

    • Oats
      Soy Milk
      Water
      Apple
      Cinnamon
      Ground Linseed
      Peanut Butter

    • Steps

    • Wash tools and apple
      Add 100g oats
      Add 15g~ ground linseed
      Add 300ml~ soy milk
      Add a few hundred mls more of water to taste
      Chop apple into small chunks and add to bowl
      Add a small spoonful of peanut butter
      Add a moderate sprinkle of cinnamon
      Stir and put in fridge over night
